Professional Heating Solutions

When your heat goes off, you need an expert fast. The 247 HomeFix Experts team of Gas Safe registered engineers is on call round the clock for emergency repairs, installations, and servicing. We offer comprehensive heating services including emergency repairs, annual servicing and energy-saving advice to help reduce bills.

What We Cover

  • Emergency Boiler Repair: Your local experts for a sudden breakdown, low pressure, or fault code. We offer all emergency diagnostics.
  • New Boiler Installation: We recommend and install A-rated combi, system, and regular boilers.
  • Landlord Gas Safety Certificates: Fully certified and fast-turnaround CP12 reports for your rental properties.
  • Annual Boiler Servicing: Protect your warranty and your home with a thorough service by our experts.
  • Power Flushing: Expert system cleaning to restore heat and efficiency.
  • Thermostat & Controls: Upgrade to a smart thermostat (like Hive or Nest) with our professional installation service.
  • Radiator Installation & Repairs: New radiator installations and repairs for optimal heating
  • Underfloor Heating: Professional installation and maintenance of underfloor heating systems
